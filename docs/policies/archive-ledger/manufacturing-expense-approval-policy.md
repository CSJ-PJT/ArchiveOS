# Manufacturing Expense Approval Policy

This is a synthetic policy document for ArchiveOS portfolio demonstration. It does not represent real financial policy.

Manufacturing expenses generated from operational systems require PM review when the expense is linked to high-severity maintenance, quality claims, emergency purchase, or vendor payment hold conditions.

Approval guidance:

- Expenses at or above 3,000,000 KRW require PM approval.
- HIGH or CRITICAL severity events require PM approval regardless of amount.
- ArchiveOS records the approval decision and policy evidence.
- Archive-Ledger remains responsible for settlement and reconciliation state transitions.
