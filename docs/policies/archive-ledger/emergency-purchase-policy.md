# Emergency Purchase Policy

This is a synthetic policy document for ArchiveOS portfolio demonstration. It does not represent real financial policy.

Emergency purchase requests require explicit approval when operational urgency is HIGH or CRITICAL, or when the amount exceeds 3,000,000 KRW.

ArchiveOS records:

- synthetic transaction identifier
- policy question
- policy evidence
- PM/Admin decision
- callback status

No real card, account, resident registration, phone, or personal data should be included.
