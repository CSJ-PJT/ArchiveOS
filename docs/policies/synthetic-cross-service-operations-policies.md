# Synthetic Cross-service Operations Policies

This is a synthetic policy document for ArchiveOS portfolio demonstration. It does not represent real financial, logistics, manufacturing, legal, or compliance policy.

## Cross-service control rule

Nexus domain events, Logistics route/cost events, and Ledger transactions must remain independently recoverable. A downstream service outage must not terminate ArchiveOS.

## Settlement isolation

- Manufacturing events are generated by Archive-Nexus.
- Route, ETA, delay, and cost are calculated by Archive-Logistics.
- Synthetic transaction, ledger, settlement, and reconciliation are owned by Archive-Ledger.
- Approval, evidence, audit, notification, and callback retry are owned by ArchiveOS.

## Human approval rule

High-value, critical, delayed, cold-chain, or reconciliation-risk events require policy evidence and human decision before downstream settlement readiness.
